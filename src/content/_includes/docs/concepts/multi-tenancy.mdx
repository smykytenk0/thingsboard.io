Defines who owns what and how users access the system.

- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/ui/tenants/'>Tenant:</DocLink> Top-level entity representing a company or business unit. Owns all resources and defines the primary security boundary.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/ui/customer/'>Customer:</DocLink> Logical sub-organization used to isolate resources (devices, dashboards) for specific end-users.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/ui/users/'>Users:</DocLink> Authenticated account with permissions. Can belong to either a Tenant or a Customer.

<Aside type="tip">
Use **Customers** to securely delegate access. A Customer User can only interact with entities specifically assigned to their Customer account.
</Aside>
