The same Generic role grants different access depending on where it is assigned.
Assigned at **Tenant** level it covers all entities in the tenant tree; assigned at **Customer** level it covers only that customer and its sub-customers.

**Prerequisites:** Basic familiarity with <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/roles'>Roles and RBAC</DocLink>.

## Scenario

**Users**
- **Bob** — member of *Tenant Admins* group (Tenant level)
- **Alice** — member of *Customer Admins* group (Customer B level)

**Devices**
- *Device A1* — owned by Tenant A
- *Device B1* — owned by Customer B

<ImageGallery images={[{ src: '/src/assets/schemas/generic-role.svg', alt: 'Generic role scope: Bob (Tenant Admins, role at Tenant level) reaches Device A1 and Device B1; Alice (Customer Admins, role at Customer B level) only reaches Device B1.', caption: 'The same Generic role assigned at different levels produces different effective scopes — Tenant-wide for Bob, Customer-only for Alice.' }]} />

**Objective**
- Bob: full access to all entities within Tenant A, including all customers and sub-customers.
- Alice: full access only within Customer B and its sub-customers.

## Step 1. Create the role

Create one role used by both assignments:

<Steps>
1. Navigate to **Security &#8702; Roles**.
2. Click **+ Add role**.
3. **Name:** *Full Access* — **Role type:** **Generic**.
4. Add one permission entry: **Resource** *All*, **Operations** *All*.
5. Click **Add**.
</Steps>

## Step 2. Assign at Tenant level (Bob)

<Steps>
1. Navigate to **Users &#8702; Groups**.
2. Open **Tenant Admins** group details &#8702; **Roles** tab.
3. Click **Add** — select **Role type** *Generic*, **Role** *Full Access*.
4. Click **Add**.
</Steps>

Bob can now perform any operation on all entities in Tenant A, including entities under all customers and sub-customers.

## Step 3. Assign at Customer level (Alice)

<Steps>
1. Navigate to **Customers** &#8702; click **Manage customer users** for Customer B.
2. Open the **Groups** tab &#8702; open **Customer Admins** details &#8702; **Roles** tab.
3. Click **Add** — select **Role type** *Generic*, **Role** *Full Access*.
4. Click **Add**.
</Steps>

Alice can now perform any operation on entities that belong to Customer B and its sub-customers only.

## Result

- Bob sees *Device A1* and *Device B1*.
- Alice sees *Device B1* only.
- Both have the same role — scope is determined by assignment level, not role definition.
