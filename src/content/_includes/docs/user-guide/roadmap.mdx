## ThingsBoard AI Ecosystem

We are building a set of AI assistants that take you from a real-world problem to a running IoT solution, step by step.

### Available now

**AI Assistant for Calculated Fields** — describe the metric you need in plain language, and the assistant builds the calculation for you. It analyzes your devices, telemetry, and asset hierarchy to produce the right formula automatically.

> *"Calculate hourly energy consumption based on power meter readings"*

**AI Assistant for Alarm Rules** — define alerting conditions in natural language. The assistant translates your intent into a complete alarm rule — threshold, condition, severity level — wired up in seconds.

> *"Trigger a warning alarm when hourly consumption exceeds 100 kWh"*

Two assistants, one coherent workflow: raw meter data → meaningful KPIs → intelligent alerts.

### Coming soon

**AI Solution Builder** — describe a real-world scenario and get a complete ThingsBoard Digital Twin model:

> *"Create an energy monitoring solution for a multi-tenant commercial building"*

The builder maps your requirements to:
- Device and asset profiles (meters, floors, buildings, tenants)
- Relations between all entities
- Calculated fields and alarm rules out of the box
- Sample customers, users, and roles for immediate demo

Blank slate → complete solution architecture, in minutes.

### Later this year

- **Dashboard AI Assistant** — create and improve dashboards through conversation, whether you built them or the AI did
- **AI Device Emulators** — simulate overload events, meter failures, and edge cases to stress-test your solution before it goes live
- **Custom Micro-Services Framework** — extend the platform with your own logic, written quickly using AI code assistants
